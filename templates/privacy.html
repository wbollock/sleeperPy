Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Introduction

SleeperPy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our fantasy football analysis tool.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data (only your Sleeper username via a cookie), don't sell your information, and don't use invasive tracking.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Sleeper Username: You provide your Sleeper username to access your fantasy football data

Automatically Collected Information

  • Cookies: We store your Sleeper username in a cookie to remember you on return visits (expires after 30 days)
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs including IP address, browser type, and access times
  • Anonymous Usage Metrics: We collect basic analytics (page views, feature usage) using privacy-focused tools with no cross-site tracking

Third-Party Data

  • Sleeper API: We fetch your league and roster data from Sleeper's public API
  • Boris Chen Tiers: We fetch player tier rankings from Boris Chen's website
  • DynastyProcess: We fetch dynasty player values from DynastyProcess CSV data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your fantasy football leagues and rosters
  • Provide tier-based player rankings and recommendations
  • Remember your username for convenience on return visits
  • Improve our service through anonymous usage analytics
  • Monitor and fix technical issues

Data Retention

  • Cookies: Stored for 30 days or until you clear them
  • Server Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging
  • Fetched League Data: Temporarily cached (15 minutes to 24 hours) to reduce API load, then discarded

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Sleeper API: We send your username to Sleeper to fetch your league data (required for functionality)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

We never:

  • Sell your data to advertisers
  • Share your data with data brokers
  • Use invasive cross-site tracking
  • Inject advertising or affiliate links

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you (though we store very little)
  • Deletion: Clear your cookie or request deletion of any stored data
  • Opt-Out: Disable cookies in your browser or use private browsing

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We recommend not sharing sensitive information through our service.

Note: Your Sleeper league data may be visible to anyone who knows your Sleeper username, as we fetch it from Sleeper's public API.

Children's Privacy

SleeperPy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you are accessing SleeperPy from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Union: We comply with GDPR principles including data minimization, purpose limitation, and your rights to access and deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/wbollock/sleeperPy/issues

Third-Party Services

SleeperPy uses the following third-party services:

  • Sleeper API: https://sleeper.com - see their Privacy Policy
  • Boris Chen Tiers: https://www.borischen.co
  • DynastyProcess: https://dynastyprocess.com
